The 'Kisan Rail' is a weekly service that will depart from Deolali, Nashik every Friday at 11 am and reach Danapur, Bihar the next day at 6.45 pm
The train will cover a distance of 1,519 kms in 31.45 hours on a single trip and will halt at Nashik Road, Manmad, Jalgaon, Bhusaval, Burhanpur, Khandwa, Itarsi, Jabalpur, Satna,
Katni, Manikpur, Prayagraj Chheoki, Pandit Deen Dayal Upadhyay junction and Buxar stations.
